About Me

Hey there, my name is Neetu. I grew up in the suburbs of Houston and moved to Austin to start school in Austin back in 2010, and have been here since. For three years, I managed the social media and WordPress accounts for a small company that collects educational data and submits it to start department education agencies. I decided I wanted to go back to school and pick up a new skill set to better myself and further my professional career. I love the outdoors, hanging out by the river and lake, and going on adventures with my pup. I studied Magazine Journalism when I was in college and enjoy keeping up with the news and politics. I'm very new to coding, and it's definitely been an adventure. A frustrating, but rewarding adventure to say the least.
